About MIB Network

MIB (Muslim in Business) is a global network of Muslim entrepreneurs and professionals committed to ethical business practices, collective support, and community development.

Referrals

Our members actively refer business to each other, creating a powerful ecosystem of trust and collaboration.

Collaboration

We believe in using business as a force for good, creating positive change in our communities and beyond.

Global Network

With chapters in over 50 countries, connect with Muslim entrepreneurs worldwide.

Growth

We conduct all business dealings with honesty and transparency, upholding the highest ethical standards as prescribed in Islam.
