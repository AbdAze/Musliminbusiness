Terms & Conditions

Please read and agree to our membership terms before proceeding.

1. Membership Agreement
By joining MIB (Muslim in Business), you agree to uphold the ethical values and principles of the community. Membership is subject to approval by chapter leadership.

2. Code of Conduct
All members are expected to conduct business with honesty, integrity, and transparency in accordance with Islamic business ethics. Any conduct found to be dishonest or harmful to the community may result in membership termination.

3. Privacy Policy
MIB respects your privacy. Your personal information will only be used for membership purposes and will not be sold or shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law.

4. Fees and Payments
The annual membership fee is ₹4,999. This fee is non-refundable once membership has been activated. Fees are subject to annual review and members will be notified 30 days in advance of any changes.

5. Referral Ethics
Members who provide referrals are responsible for ensuring the quality and integrity of those referrals. False or misleading referrals may result in membership suspension.

6. Meeting Attendance
Members are encouraged to attend weekly chapter meetings regularly. Consistent absence without notice may affect membership standing.

7. Termination
MIB reserves the right to terminate membership for violations of the Code of Conduct, non-payment, or other reasons at the discretion of chapter leadership, with or without refund.
