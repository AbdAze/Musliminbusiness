About WE

WE (Women Entrepreneurs) is a dedicated platform within MIB (Muslims in Business) that celebrates, empowers, and uplifts Muslim women who are passionate about business, leadership, and innovation. We believe that women have the power to shape industries, inspire communities, and lead with purpose.

At WE, our mission is to:

1. Support Muslim women in their entrepreneurial journey with mentorship, resources, and networking.
2. Encourage leadership, creativity, and financial independence among women from all walks of life.
3. Create a safe and inclusive space where Muslim women entrepreneurs can thrive, collaborate, and share their stories.

Whether you're a startup founder, a home-based business owner, a professional freelancer, or dreaming of launching your venture — WE is here to help you grow, connect, and succeed.

Together, we rise. Together, we build. Together, we lead.
