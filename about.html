Our Story

From a Small
Gathering to a Movement

Founded in 2024, Muslims in Business (MIB) began as a small gathering of Muslim entrepreneurs in Surat, Gujarat, India who wanted to create a supportive business community grounded in Islamic values.

What started as a local initiative has now grown into a network with chapters in over 3 states in India, connecting hundreds of Muslim business professionals.

Our mission is to empower Muslim entrepreneurs to thrive while maintaining the highest ethical standards in their business practices.

Our Core Values

Principles That Guide Us

01

Trust, Integrity & Unity

We conduct all business dealings with honesty and transparency, upholding the highest ethical standards as prescribed in Islam.

02

Mutual Support

Our members actively support one another through business referrals, collaborations, and knowledge sharing — a true brotherhood.

03

Collaboration

We believe in using business as a force for good, creating positive change in our communities and beyond our borders.

04

Growth

Continuous improvement — personal, professional, and spiritual — is at the heart of everything we do at MIB.

Our Structure

How MIB Works

01

Weekly Meetings

Members meet weekly to share business updates, exchange referrals, and learn from guest speakers.

02

Exclusive Seats

Only one member per professional category, ensuring no competition within chapters.

03

Structured Referrals

Our proven system helps members generate quality business referrals consistently.

04

Social Responsibility

Members contribute to community initiatives reflecting Islamic social values.
